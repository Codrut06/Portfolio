Performance Indicators

Mean Time to Repair (MTTR) A performance indicator that measures the average time required to fix a fault in equipment or a system, thus evaluating the efficiency of the repair process.

Work Efficiency This indicator measures the efficiency of work in ship repairs, expressing the ratio between tasks and the time/effort allocated.

Labour Productivity This indicator measures the efficiency of machinery usage in ship repairs, reporting the volume of work done against the number of hours allocated.

Delivery On Time Rate This indicator evaluates the efficiency of machinery usage in ship repairs, calculated as the ratio between work volume and allocated hours.

Capacity Utilization This indicator evaluates the percentage of time that resources are utilized during ship repairs, useful for assessing the efficiency of docks or other facilities. A high rate indicates effective planning and management of the work.

Repeat Repairs This indicator evaluates the frequency of the same fault or category of faults reappearing after a repair. A high rate may indicate issues with the quality of work or diagnostic processes.
